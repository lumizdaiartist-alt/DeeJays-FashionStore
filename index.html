The Dee Jay Story

At Dee Jay's Fashion Store, we cater to the modern Nigerian man who wants to lead the boardroom in a crisp Senator suit on Monday, and dominate the streets in baggy cargos and fresh Nikes on Friday.

Our Native wears are crafted with the famous "7-star" wool, ensuring that the fabric doesn't fade or wear out easily. For our streetwear, we source only authentic sneakers and high-GSM cotton for our shirts. Quality is our signature.

Located in the heart of Lagos, we ship nationwide. 100% Proudly Nigerian.
